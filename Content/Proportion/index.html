    Proportion

    A proportion is defined as an equation that states that two ratios are equal.

    Symbols and Terminology

    • $\cfrac{a}{b}\quad$ Antecedent
    • $\cfrac{c}{d}\quad$ Consequent
    • $b,c\quad$ Means terms
    • $a,d\quad$ Extremes terms

    Notation

    • Formal Definition: A proportion is a relationship of the form: $$\cfrac{a}{b}=\cfrac{c}{d} \qquad b,d\neq0$$

    Properties

    Applying the Properties of Equality to a generic proportion $\cfrac{{\textcolor{red}a}}{{\textcolor{green}b}} = \cfrac{{\textcolor{blue}c}}{{\textcolor{violet}d}}$:

    • Cross-Multiplication Property: Often used to solve proportions for unknown values: $${\color{red}a}{\color{violet}d}={\color{green}b}{\color{blue}c}$$
    • Reciprocal Property: the reciprocals are also equal: $$\cfrac{{\color{green}b}}{{\color{red}a}} = \cfrac{{\color{violet}d}}{{\color{blue}c}}$$
    • Alternando Properties (Means): you can switch the means: $$\cfrac{{\color{red}a}}{{\color{blue}c}} = \cfrac{{\color{green}b}}{{\color{violet}d}}$$
    • Alternando Properties (Extremes): you can switch the extremes: $$\cfrac{{\color{violet}d}}{{\color{green}b}} = \cfrac{{\color{blue}c}}{{\color{red}a}}$$
    • Componendo Property: then the ratio of the sum of the numerator and denominator to the denominator is also equal:$$\cfrac{{\color{red}a}+{\color{green}b}}{{\color{green}b}} = \cfrac{{\color{blue}c}+{\color{violet}d}}{{\color{violet}d}}$$
    • Dividendo Property: the ratio of the difference of the numerator and denominator to the denominator is also equal: $$\cfrac{{\color{red}a}-{\color{green}b}}{{\color{green}b}} = \cfrac{{\color{blue}c}-{\color{violet}d}}{{\color{violet}d}}$$
    • Componendo and Dividendo: It relates a proportion involving sums and differences to a simpler proportion: $$\cfrac{{\color{red}a}+{\color{green}b}}{{{\color{red}a}-\color{green}b}} = \cfrac{{\color{blue}c}+{\color{violet}d}}{{\color{blue}c}-{\color{violet}d}}$$
