    Dynamics

    Dynamics is the branch of mechanics that studies the forces and their effects on the motion of objects. It focuses on understanding how forces cause changes in the velocity and motion of bodies, typically using concepts like acceleration, force, mass, and energy. The fundamental laws of dynamics are governed by Newton's laws of motion, which describe the relationship between an object's motion and the forces acting on it.
