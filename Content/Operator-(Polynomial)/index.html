    Addition and Subtraction Similitude

    The processes are nearly identical, with subtraction requiring an additional step to compute the additive inverse of the subtrahend polynomial $Q(x)$.

    Steps for addition and subtraction

    1. Prepare the polynomials:
      Arrange the polynomials in line, using the inverse of $Q$ for subtraction.
    2. Identify like terms:
      Look for terms with the same degree.
    3. Combine coefficients:
      Add or subtract the coefficients of the like terms.
    4. Simplify:
      Write the resulting terms as a single polynomial.

    Examples

    $Q(x,y)=-12x+15xy-12-x$
