    Special Products

    Special Products are algebraic expressions resulting from the application of specific patterns or formulas to the multiplication of Polynomial.
    These patterns simplify operations by providing ready-to-use identities that describe the expansion or factorization of certain types of polynomial products.
    Special products are particularly useful in simplifying polynomial expressions and solving equations efficiently.
