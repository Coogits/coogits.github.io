    Algebra

    Algebra is a branch of mathematics that focuses on symbols and the rules for manipulating them. It is used to express mathematical relationships, solve equations, and model real-world situations. Algebra includes simple tasks, like solving for unknowns, and extends to advanced topics such as abstract algebra, which explores structures like groups, rings, and fields. Algebra plays a crucial role in many fields, including science, engineering, economics, and beyond.
