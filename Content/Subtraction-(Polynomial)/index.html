    Subtraction (Polynomial)

    Subtraction (Polynomial): Is the operation between two polynomial performed by subtracting the corresponding terms of the polynomials, just as in addition. To subtract two polynomials, we subtract the coefficients of the terms with the same degree.

    Symbols

    • $P(x)\quad$ Its a Polynomials and the minuend of the subtraction.
    • $Q(x)\quad$ Its a Polynomials and the subtrahend of the subtraction.

    Notation

    • Basic Form:
      Can be viewed as adding the additive inverse (or the reciprocal) of the subtrahend $Q(x)$: $$P(x) - Q(x) \quad\to\quad P(x)+(-Q(x))$$

    Steps for Subtraction

    Algorithm

    1. Prepare the polynomials:
      The subtraction between two polynomials $P(x)-Q(x)$ can be converted into an addiction: $$\begin{align}&\text{subtraction}\quad&\to\quad&\text{addiction}\\ &P(x)=ax\pm cy \quad&\to\quad &P(x)=ax\pm cy \\ &Q(x)=\mp bx\mp dy \quad&\to\quad &-Q(x)=\pm bx\pm dy \end{align}$$ Arrange the polynomials in line. $$ax\pm bx\pm cy\pm dy\pm\ldots$$
    2. Identify like terms:
      Look for terms with the same degree. $$\underline{ax}\pm \underline{bx}\pm \underline{\underline{cy}}\pm \underline{\underline{dy}}\pm\ldots$$
    3. Combine coefficients:
      Add or subtract the coefficients of the like terms. $$(a\pm b)x\pm (c\pm d)y\pm\ldots$$
    4. Simplify:
      Write the resulting terms as a single polynomial. $$Ax\pm By\pm\ldots\qquad \text{where }\begin{align}A=a\pm b \\ B=c\pm d\end{align}$$

    Algorithm Examples

    Steps example 1
    $P(x,y)=2x+y$
    $Q(x,y)=x-7y$
    $P(x,y)-Q(x,y)$
    1. Prepare the polynomials: $2x+y-x+7y$
    2. Identify like terms: $\underline{2x}+\underline{\underline{y}}-\underline{x}+\underline{\underline{7y}}$
    3. Combine coefficients: $(2-1)x+(1+7)y$
    4. Simplify: $x+8y$
