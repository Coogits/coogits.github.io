    Welcome to My Digital Portfolio

    Welcome to my personal corner of the web, a place where I share my journey through programming, science, and creativity. This site serves as a collection of my organized notes, projects, and ideas, reflecting my ongoing exploration of mathematics, physics, computer science, and engineering. I strive to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications.

    My Journey in Programming

    I started my programming journey back in 2010 with the C programming language. This was my entry point into the world of coding, and it ignited a lasting passion. Over time, my curiosity led me to dive deeper into the assembly language, where I learned how a computer truly operates at a low level. From there, I explored multiple programming languages, algorithms, and data structures, refining my skills for diverse projects.

    While my early focus was on back-end development, I later expanded to front-end scripting, web development, and other aspects of programming, all driven by a desire to solve problems and create functional, efficient systems.

    My Passion for Game Development and Arduino

    A significant part of my journey involves game development with Unity and Unreal Engine. I enjoy combining my technical skills with my love for art to create immersive, interactive experiences. The ability to merge coding with creative expression is something that deeply excites me.

    Alongside game development, I'm passionate about Arduino and electronics. Building hardware projects has taught me a great deal about the physical world and how to integrate software with tangible objects. Arduino allows me to experiment with sensors, motors, and microcontrollers, expanding my skillset beyond the digital world and fostering a deeper understanding of how things work.

    The Influence of Science and Physics

    I have always held great respect for the scientific method, particularly in the fields of physics and mathematics. These disciplines are the backbone of problem-solving and innovation in programming. Understanding the principles of physics helps me create more optimized, efficient systems, whether it's in game physics, algorithms, or even hardware projects with Arduino. The rigorous nature of scientific inquiry encourages me to approach every problem with critical thinking and precision, always striving for the most effective and well-thought-out solutions.

    This Site

    This website is a growing collection of my work, offering insights into the projects I've completed, the lessons I've learned, and my continued journey of discovery. It's designed as both a portfolio and a place to share knowledge, reflecting my commitment to improving and expanding my skillset over time. As I progress, I plan to continuously update and enrich the content here.
