    Complement (Arithmetic)

    The Complement Method is a arithmetical operation used to simplify subtraction by transforming it into addition. This is commonly applied in computer science and mathematics, particularly in base $b$ numeral systems (e.g., binary, decimal). The complement method relies on two types of complements:

    Symbols

    • $C_{b-1}(N)\quad$ Diminished Radix Complement
    • $C_b(N)\quad$ Radix Complement
    • $N\quad$ Is the original number
    • $b\quad$ Is the base of $N$.
    • $n\quad$ Is the number of digits in $N$.

    Formulas

    • Radix Complement:
      The complement calculated by subtracting $N$ from $b^n$. This is the general complement used across numeral systems. $$C_b(N)=b^n-N$$
    • Two's Complement:
      It's the radix complement with base 2. $$C_2(N)=\mbox{NOT}(N)+1$$
    • Diminished Radix Complement:
      Calculated by subtracting the number $N$ from $(b^n-1)$. $$C_{b-1}(N)=(b^n-1)-N$$
    • Binary Diminished Radix Complement:
      the Diminished Radix Complement with base 2, is equivalent to bitwise $\mbox{NOT}$ of $N$. $$C_{2-1}(N)=\mbox{NOT}(N)$$

    Subtraction Method

    To perform $A-B$, follow these steps:

    1. Compute the complement of $B$ (in this case is applied the Radix Complement, for binary number apply the Two's Complement, the Diminished Radix Complement can be applied too but the carry would be applied differently) .
      1. $C_b(B)=b^n-B$
    2. Add $A$ to the complement of $B$.
      1. $S=A+C_b(B)$
    3. Handle the carry:
      • If $S\geq b^n$ (a carry is produced), discard the carry:
        • $\textbf{Result: }S-b^n$
      • if $S<b^n$ (no carry is produced) the result is
        • $\textbf{Result: }S$

    Complement Method for Subtraction: Example in Base 2 (Binary)

    $1011_b-0110_b$

    1. Compute the complement of B (Two's Complement for binary).
      1. $C_2(B)=\mbox{NOT}(B)+1$
        1. $C_2(0110_2)=\mbox{NOT}(0110_b)+1=1010_b$
    2. Add $A$ to the complement of $B$.
      1. $S=A+C_b(B)$
        1. $S=1011_b+1010_b=10101_b$
    3. discard the carry $S\geq 2^4$:
      • $\textbf{Result: }S-b^n$
        • $\textbf{Result: }10101_b-1000_b=0101_b$
