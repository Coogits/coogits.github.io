    Formulary (Plane Geometry)

    Triangle (Generic)

    Triangle

    Concept Formula
    Perimeter $2p=a+b+c$
    Area (Generic) $\mathcal{A}=\frac{1}{2}\times\text{base}\times\text{height}$
    Heron's Formula $\mathcal{A}=\sqrt{p(p-a)(p-b)(p-c)}$
    Circumradius $R=\cfrac{abc}{4\mathcal{A}}$
    Inradius $r=\cfrac{\mathcal{A}}{p}$
    Median Point $M_a=\cfrac{B+C}{2}$
    Centroid $G=\cfrac{ A+B+C}{3}$
    Law of Sines $2R=\dfrac{a}{\sin\alpha}=\dfrac{b}{\sin\beta}=\dfrac{c}{\sin\gamma}$
    Law of Cosines $c^2=a^2+b^2-2ab\cos\gamma$

    Right Triangle

    Right Triangle

    Concept Formula
    Right Triangle $\mathcal{A}=\cfrac{ab}{2}$
    height $h=\cfrac{ab}{c}$
    Circumradius $R=m_c=\cfrac{c}{2}$
    Inradius $r=\cfrac{a+b-c}{2}$
    Legs $\begin{align} \alpha=c\sin\alpha=c\cos\beta \\ b=c\sin\beta=c\cos\alpha\end{align}$
    Angle Sum $\alpha+\beta=90^\circ$
    Law of Sines $\cfrac{a}{\sin\alpha}=\cfrac{b}{\sin\beta}=c$
    Pythagorean Theorem $c^2=a^2+b^2$

    Circle

    Circle (Euclidean Geometry)

    Concept Formula
    Radius $R=\sqrt{(x-x_0)^2+(y-y_0)^2}$
    Diameter $d=2R$
    Perimeter $2p=2\pi R$
    Area $\mathcal{A}=\pi R^2$
    Arc Lenght $L=R\theta$
    Sector Area $S = \frac{1}{2} R^2 \theta$
    Chord Lenght $C=2R\sin\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)$
