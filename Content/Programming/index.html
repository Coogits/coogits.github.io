    Programming

    Programming is the process of creating software (or a program) by writing, testing, and maintaining a set of instructions that a computer can execute to perform specific tasks. It involves designing algorithms, structuring data, and utilizing programming languages to translate human ideas and problem-solving strategies into a form that a computer can understand and act upon. Through programming, developers bring ideas to life, creating applications, tools, websites, and systems that fulfill a variety of functions and solve real-world problems.
