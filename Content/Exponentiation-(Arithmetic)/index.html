    Exponentiation

    Exponentiation is the arithmetical operation of raising a number (the base) to the power of an exponent, indicating how many times the base is multiplied by itself.

    Symbols

    • $a\quad$ Base; the quantity multiplied by itself.
    • $n\quad$ Exponent; how many times the base is multiplied by itself.
    • $c\quad$ Power; the result of the exponential.

    Notation

    Definition

    • Basic Form Generic definition: $$a^n=b$$

    Examples

    • Quadratic Form
      With exponent value 2: $$a^2=b$$
    • Cubic Form
      With exponent value 3: $$a^3=b$$

    Properties

    • Product of a power:
      • $a^n\cdot a^m=a^{m+n}$
    • Power of a power:
      • $(a^n)^m=a^{m\cdot n}$
    • Power of product:
      • $(a\cdot b)^n=a^n\cdot b^n$
    • Power of fraction:
      • $\left(\cfrac{a}{b}\right)^n=\cfrac{a^n}{b^n}$
    • Reciprocal Exponent:
      • $a^{-n}=\left(\cfrac{1}{a}\right)^n=\cfrac{1}{a^n} \qquad a\neq0 \land n\in\mathbb{N}$
      • $\left(\cfrac{a}{b}\right)^{-n}=\left(\cfrac{b}{a}\right)^n \qquad a,b\neq0 \land n\in\mathbb{N}$
    • Fractional Exponent:
      • $a^\frac{m}{n} = \sqrt[n]{a^m}\qquad a>0 \land m,n\in\mathbb{N}$
    • Identity Element: Any nonzero number raised to the power of 0 equals 1:
      • $a^0=1\qquad a\neq0$
      • $0^0=\text{undefined}$
    • Even Exponent: Raising any real number $a$ to an even $2n$ results in a positive value, regardless of whether $a$ is positive or negative:
      • $a^{2n}>0 \qquad a\in\mathbb{R},n\in\mathbb{N}$
