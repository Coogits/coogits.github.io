    Area (Geometry)

    The area is the measure of the amount of space enclosed within a two-dimensional shape or surface.

    • Units: It is typically expressed in square units, such as square meters $m^2$

    Common Formulas

    • Rectangle: $$\mathcal{A}=length\times width$$
    • Triangle: $$\mathcal{A}=\frac{1}{2}\times\text{base}\times\text{height}$$
    • Circle (Euclidean Geometry): $$\mathcal{A}=\pi\times \text{radius}^2$$
    • Parallelogram $$\mathcal{A}=\text{base}\times\text{height}$$
    • Trapezoid: $$\mathcal{A}=\frac{1}{2}\times(\text{base}_1+\text{base}_2)\times\text{height}$$
