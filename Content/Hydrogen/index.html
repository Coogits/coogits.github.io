    Hydrogen

    Hydrogen is the first element in the periodic table, with the atomic number 1.
    It is the lightest and most abundant element in the universe, making up about 75% of all normal matter by mass.
    Each hydrogen atom consists of one proton and one electron, and in its most common isotope, no neutrons.
