    Periods

    Renaissance

    • Start: c. 1300 CE
    • End: 1600 CE
    • Key Features: The revival of classical learning and art, advances in science, and the flourishing of literature and philosophy.

    Age of Exploration

    • Start: 1400 CE
    • End: 1600 CE
    • Key Features: European exploration of the Americas, Africa, and Asia, and the beginning of global trade routes.

    Scientific Revolution

    • Start: 1543 CE
    • End: 1687 CE

    Protestant Reformation

    • Start: 1517 CE
    • End: 1648 CE
    • Key Features: The division of Christianity between Catholics and Protestants, the rise of religious wars, and the establishment of Protestant churches.
