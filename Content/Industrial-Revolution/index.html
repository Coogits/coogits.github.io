    Industrial Revolution

    The Industrial Revolution was a period of major transformation in manufacturing, technology, and society that began in the late 18th century and continued through the 19th century. It started in Britain before spreading to Europe, North America, and eventually the rest of the world.

    • Start: 1760 CE
    • End: 1840 CE
